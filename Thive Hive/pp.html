Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal and Business Information: We may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and business information when you voluntarily provide it to us through forms, registrations, or when you contact us. This information may include details about your business, industry, and marketing goals.

b. Usage Information: We automatically collect certain information about your visit to our Website and the use of our marketing services, including your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect this data to enhance your experience and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

a. Providing Marketing Services: We use your business information to deliver our digital marketing services, including but not limited to advertising, search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, and email marketing.

d. Marketing Communications: With your consent, we may send you updates, newsletters, marketing communications, and promotional materials related to our services.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal and business information to third parties under the following circumstances:

a. Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our marketing services, but only to the extent necessary to fulfill their roles.

b. Legal Obligations: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, the rights of our clients, or the rights of others.

4. Your Choices

a. You can manage your communication preferences by following the instructions provided in our marketing emails or by contacting us directly.

b. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that this may affect your experience on our Website.

5. Security

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information that our website collects, as well as how we use and protect that information.

Our Website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.
